Item 3.

Let \(A_1,\dotsc,A_N\) be planar regions with well-defined area. Suppose that \(\Area(A_i \cap A_j) = 0\) for all \(1 \leq i,j \leq N\) satisfying \(i \neq j\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \Area(A_1 \cup \cdots \cup A_N) = \Area(A_1) + \cdots + \Area(A_N)\text{.} \end{equation*}
