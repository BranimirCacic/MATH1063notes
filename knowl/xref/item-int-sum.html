Item 2.

Suppose that \(f\) and \(g\) are both integrable on \([a,b]\text{.}\) Define \(f+g\) on \([a,b]\) by \((f+g)(x) \defn f(x) + g(x)\text{.}\) Then \(f+g\) is integrable on \([a,b]\) and
\begin{equation*} \int_a^b (f+g) = \int_a^b f + \int_a^b g \end{equation*}
i.e., \(\int_a^b \left(f(x)+g(x)\right)\du{x} = \int_a^b f(x)\du{x} + \int_a^b g(x)\du{x}\text{.}\)
